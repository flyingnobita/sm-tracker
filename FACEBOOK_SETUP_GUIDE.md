# Setting up your Facebook Tracker: A Step-by-Step Guide

This guide will walk you through creating a Meta Developer account, configuring a Meta App, obtaining the exactly right permissions, and exporting a **Facebook Page Access Token** to use in `sm-tracker`.

## Step 1: Create a Meta Developer Account

_You must have a standard Facebook account to do this. This account must be an Admin of the Facebook Page you want to track._

1. **Go to the portal**: Navigate to [developers.facebook.com](https://developers.facebook.com) in your web browser.
2. **Log in**: Click **"Log In"** in the top right corner and sign in with your normal Facebook credentials.
3. **Register as a Developer**: Once logged in, click **"Get Started"** in the top right. Follow the prompt to verify your account (via phone or email) and accept the Developer Policies.

## Step 2: Create a Meta App

_Your tokens are generated by an "App". We need to create a simple one to sit between Facebook and `sm-tracker`._

1. **Go to My Apps**: Click **"My Apps"** in the top right corner of the Developer Portal.
2. **Start creation**: Click the green **"Create App"** button.
3. **Select Use Case**: Choose **"Other"**, click Next, and then choose **"Business"** (this gives you access to the Graph API). Click Next.
4. **Name the App**: Enter a name like `sm-tracker` and add your contact email. Click **"Create App"**. (You may be prompted for your Facebook password).

## Step 3: Grant the App "Advanced Access" to Public Features

**\*Crucial Step**: Without this, the Graph API will throw a `(#100) Object does not exist` error when `sm-tracker` tries to read your page's follower count.\*

1. **Navigate to App Review**: In the left-hand sidebar of your App Dashboard, expand **"App Review"** and click on **"Permissions and Features"**.
2. **Search for proper features**: In the search box on that page, search for **`Page Public Metadata Access`** (or **`Page Public Content Access`**).
3. **Request Advanced Access**: You will see it listed under the "Features" section. Click the **"Request Advanced Access"** or **"Get Advanced Access"** button next to it.
    - _Note: Because you are the creator and admin of this app, Meta will usually grant this immediately without requiring a manual human review._
4. **Verify**: Ensure the green pill icon now says **"Advanced Access"**.

## Step 4: Generate your Page Access Token

_We will use the Graph API Explorer to request a token and explicitly tie it to your Facebook Page._

1. **Open the Explorer**: Click on **"Tools"** in the top navigation bar, then select **"Graph API Explorer"**.
2. **Select your App**: On the right panel, under **"Meta App"**, ensure your newly created app (`sm-tracker`) is selected in the dropdown.
3. **Add Permissions**: In the **"Permissions"** section (below the "User or Page" dropdown), click **"Add a Permission"**.
    - Navigate to the **"Events Groups Pages"** category.
    - Select both **`pages_show_list`** and **`pages_read_engagement`**. (Do _not_ select the deprecated `manage_pages` permission, it will break the flow).
4. **Trigger Login / Token Generation**: Click the blue **"Generate Access Token"** button.
5. **Authorize the App**: A Facebook login popup will appear. Continue as your user profile.
    - **_Crucial Step_**: When it asks what pages the app can access, you MUST either leave **"Opt in to all current and future Pages"** checked, or click "Edit Settings" and specifically check the box next to your target Page (e.g., "Flying Nobita").
    - Click **"Save"** and **"Got It"** to close the popup.
6. **Switch to a Page Token**: Look back at the Graph API Explorer right panel. Click the **"User or Page"** dropdown. Instead of "User Token", you should now see your Facebook Page listed under the **"Page Access Tokens"** header.
7. **Select the Page**: Click your page's name. The long string in the "Access Token" box at the very top will immediately change to a new string starting with `EAA...`.

_Congratulations! This string is your official **Page Access Token**._

## Step 5: Configure your `.env` File

_Tell `sm-tracker` to use your new direct token._

1. **Copy the Token**: Click the copy icon next to the token string in the Graph API Explorer.
2. **Paste it**: Open your `.env` file and paste the token specifically into the `FACEBOOK_PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN` variable:

```env
FACEBOOK_PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN=EAAD0ZAleH... (your copied token)
```

1. **Finished!** Because you used the `FACEBOOK_PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN` specifically, you do **not** need to manually find or enter your numeric `FACEBOOK_ID`. The `sm-tracker` will securely look up the ID natively at runtime!
